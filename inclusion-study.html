S&P 500 Index Inclusion Effect

Analyzing the historical performance of stocks added to the S&P 500. We examine the price action before the effective date and the returns 1 week and 1 month after inclusion.

Important Data Note: Due to the proprietary nature of official "Announcement Dates" (when S&P publicly announces a change), this analysis uses the "Effective Date" (when the stock actually joins the index) as the primary anchor. To estimate the "Announcement Effect", we use the return from 5 trading days prior to the Effective Date (T-5 to T=0) as a fuzzy proxy for the announcement rally.
